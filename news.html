Art Exhibition 2023
9 March 2023

The art exhibition organized for the 10th anniversary of the college was held at the college on 09/03/2023. For that, the participation of the former Minister of Education, Member of Parliament Mr. Dallas Alahapperuma, who took the initiative to start the college, was a blessing. Also, a group of distinguished guests including Monaragala Governor General, Uva Provincial Education Secretary, Monaragala Divisional Registrar, Regional Education Director, Chairman of Deundara Regional Council, Badalkumbura Police Station Commander and School Development Committee joined us.
